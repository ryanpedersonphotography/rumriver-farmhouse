Gather Around the Table

The heart of the farmhouse beats strongest in our sun-drenched dining room, where handcrafted furniture meets panoramic views of the Rum River valley. Here, morning coffee becomes a meditation and evening meals transform into lasting memories.

With seating for eight and a rustic farm table that's hosted countless celebrations, this space invites connection. The original wood beams overhead tell stories of generations past, while modern amenities ensure every comfort for today's gatherings.

Rest in Rural Luxury

Each bedroom is a sanctuary designed for deep sleep and peaceful mornings. Wake to birdsong filtering through vintage lace curtains, with views that stretch across wildflower meadows to the forest beyond.

Premium linens, memory foam mattresses, and carefully curated antiques create an atmosphere of refined comfort. Whether you're here for adventure or escape, these rooms offer the perfect retreat after days spent exploring the untamed beauty that surrounds you.

Morning Coffee & River Views

Start your day on the wraparound porch, where the morning mist rises from the Rum River and the only sounds are birdsong and rustling leaves. This is where time slows down and memories are made.

Our thoughtfully restored farmhouse preserves the character of Minnesota's rural heritage while providing every modern convenience. Original hardwood floors, exposed beams, and vintage fixtures create an authentic atmosphere that connects you to the land's rich history.

Evening Gatherings Under the Stars

As the sun sets over the meadow, gather around the fire pit for s'mores and storytelling. The clear Minnesota sky reveals a blanket of stars rarely seen in city life, creating the perfect backdrop for unforgettable evenings.

Inside, the game room awaits with vintage board games, a poker table, and comfortable seating for the whole family. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway or a family reunion, this farmhouse adapts to your needs while maintaining its warm, welcoming spirit.
